Here at Serpiant we are dedicated to sustaining and preserving dead audio video formats. In particular we are focusing on reprinting and releasing old Laser Disc movies fully restored and remastered. Not a lot of people know that Laser Disc is a purely Analog format except for DTS sound and the like. We would love to see Laser Disc quality taken to it's most extreme with lossless composite video!
