Page Structure and Layout

I you would like to see the reference I used Click Here.

Mostly I focused on the navigation pane at the top of the screen. I wanted to make the page easier to both navigate and grade. In order for me to accomplish this I fixed the Navigation bar in place so the user can always click on links he would like to visit. As it says in the reading, "Headers provide site identity and global navigation, with search and perhaps other tools." With that in mind I redesigned the Serpiant logo made it a link to the home page and put several useful links underneath it on the left side. I also started work on the content section of the page making the headers on the content more noticeable and I feel more aesthetically pleasing.

I feel that my example adheres to basics of site structure because it is easier to navigate through. I also feel that getting rid of the distracting backgrounds allows the page to breath more freely.
